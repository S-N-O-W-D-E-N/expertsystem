Tips for Managing Sunburn

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water to help hydrate your skin and prevent dehydration caused by sunburn.

Apply Cool Compresses

Use cool (not cold) compresses on the sunburned areas to reduce inflammation and soothe the skin.

Avoid Further Sun Exposure

Stay out of the sun while your skin heals to prevent worsening of the burn.

Treatment Options

Aloe Vera

Apply aloe vera gel to sunburned skin to reduce redness and promote healing.

Over-the-Counter Creams

Use hydrocortisone cream or pain-relieving ointments to alleviate inflammation and discomfort.

Hydrating Lotions

Moisturizing lotions or ointments that contain ingredients like vitamin E can help restore moisture to damaged skin.

General Advice

Protect Your Skin in the Future

Wear sunscreen with at least SPF 30, even on cloudy days, to protect your skin from UV damage.

Wear Loose Clothing

Cover sunburned skin with loose, soft fabrics to reduce irritation.

Consult a Doctor

If your sunburn is severe, blisters form, or you experience intense pain, consult a healthcare provider for medical advice.
