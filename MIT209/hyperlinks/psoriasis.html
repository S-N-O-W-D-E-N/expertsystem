Tips for Managing Psoriasis

Moisturize Regularly

Use thick, fragrance-free moisturizers to help reduce dryness and itching.

Manage Stress

Practice stress management techniques such as meditation or yoga, as stress can trigger flare-ups.

Avoid Triggers

Identify and avoid common triggers like cold weather, smoking, or certain medications.

Treatment Options

Topical Treatments

Apply corticosteroid creams or vitamin D analogs to help reduce symptoms.

Phototherapy

Light therapy using ultraviolet (UV) light can help reduce scaling and inflammation.

Systemic Treatments

For severe cases, doctors may recommend oral medications or biologics to suppress the immune response.

General Advice

Maintain a Healthy Diet

Eat foods rich in antioxidants, such as fruits and vegetables, and avoid inflammatory foods like processed meats.

Stay Hydrated

Drinking plenty of water helps keep your skin hydrated and promotes overall health.

Consult a Dermatologist

If your psoriasis worsens or affects your quality of life, seek professional advice for advanced treatments.
