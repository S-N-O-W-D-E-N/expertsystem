Tips for Managing Hives

Avoid Known Triggers

Identify and avoid triggers such as certain foods, medications, or stress.

Keep Skin Cool

Apply cool compresses to affected areas to reduce itching and swelling.

Wear Loose Clothing

Choose soft, breathable fabrics to minimize irritation.

Treatment Options

Antihistamines

Take over-the-counter or prescribed antihistamines to relieve symptoms.

Topical Creams

Use anti-itch creams or lotions to soothe irritation.

Epinephrine for Severe Cases

For severe allergic reactions causing hives, an epinephrine injection may be required.

General Advice

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water to help your skin stay healthy and recover faster.

Monitor Symptoms

Keep track of the frequency and severity of your hives to share with your doctor.

Seek Medical Advice

If symptoms persist or worsen, consult a healthcare professional promptly.
