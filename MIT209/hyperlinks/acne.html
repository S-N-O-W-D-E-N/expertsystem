Tips for Managing Acne

Cleanse Gently

Wash your face twice a day with a mild cleanser to remove excess oil and dirt.

Avoid Touching Your Face

Keep your hands away from your face to prevent transferring oils and bacteria.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water to help keep your skin clear and balanced.

Treatment Options

Topical Treatments

Use creams or gels containing benzoyl peroxide, salicylic acid, or retinoids.

Oral Medications

Consult a doctor for oral antibiotics or hormone therapy if needed for severe cases.

Facial Peels

Chemical peels can help reduce acne and prevent future breakouts.

General Advice

Maintain a Healthy Diet

Include foods rich in antioxidants, like fruits and vegetables, to promote clear skin.

Don't Over-Exfoliate

Exfoliating too frequently can irritate the skin and worsen acne.

Consult a Dermatologist

If your acne doesn't improve, seek professional advice for tailored treatment options.
