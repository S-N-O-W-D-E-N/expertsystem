Tips for Managing Eczema

Keep Skin Moisturized

Apply a thick moisturizer twice daily to keep skin hydrated.

Avoid Triggers

Identify and avoid common triggers like allergens, stress, and irritants.

Use Gentle Products

Opt for hypoallergenic and fragrance-free skincare products.

Treatment Options

Topical Steroids

Use corticosteroid creams for reducing inflammation and itching.

Antihistamines

Take antihistamines to relieve severe itching, especially at night.

Phototherapy

Consider light therapy for chronic or severe eczema cases.

General Advice

Maintain a Balanced Diet

Include anti-inflammatory foods like fish and leafy greens.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water to keep your skin healthy and hydrated.

Consult a Dermatologist

If symptoms persist or worsen, seek professional advice.
