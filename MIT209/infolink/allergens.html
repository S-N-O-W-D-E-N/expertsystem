Exposed to Allergen

Allergens are substances that can trigger an allergic reaction. Exposure occurs when the body comes into contact with allergens like pollen, dust, or certain foods.

Common Allergens:

  • Pollen from trees and flowers
  • Dust mites
  • Pet dander
  • Foods like nuts, shellfish, or dairy
  • Insect stings

Symptoms of Allergen Exposure:

Common symptoms include sneezing, itching, watery eyes, swelling, or difficulty breathing. Consult a doctor if symptoms persist or worsen.
