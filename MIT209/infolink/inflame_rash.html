Inflamed Rash

An inflamed rash appears as red, swollen skin often accompanied by bumps, itching, and irritation. It can be caused by infections, allergies, or chronic skin conditions.

Common Causes:

  • Allergic reactions to food, medication, or skincare products
  • Skin infections such as eczema or psoriasis
  • Contact dermatitis from irritants or poison ivy
  • Heat rashes caused by excessive sweating
  • Infections from bacteria, fungi, or viruses

Treatment for Inflamed Rash:

Inflamed rashes are treated based on their cause. It can include anti-inflammatory creams, antihistamines, avoiding triggers, or prescribed medications for chronic conditions. See a doctor for persistent cases.
