Scaly Rash or Flaky Texture

What is a Scaly Rash?

A scaly rash is characterized by dry, flaky patches of skin that may peel off. It can occur due to conditions like eczema, psoriasis, or fungal infections. These rashes may appear red, itchy, and inflamed, causing discomfort. Treatment often involves moisturizing, medications, or addressing the underlying cause.
