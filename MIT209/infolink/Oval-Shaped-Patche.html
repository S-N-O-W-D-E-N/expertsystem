Oval-Shaped Patches of Red, Inflamed Skin

Oval-shaped patches of red, inflamed skin are often a sign of conditions like eczema, ringworm, or other dermatological issues. These patches may be itchy, tender, or inflamed, and can appear in various sizes and shapes.

Common Causes:

  • Atopic dermatitis (eczema)
  • Ringworm or fungal infections
  • Psoriasis (chronic skin condition)
  • Contact dermatitis (skin irritation from allergens or irritants)
  • Insect bites or other skin reactions
