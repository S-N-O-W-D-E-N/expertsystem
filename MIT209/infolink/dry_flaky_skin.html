Dry Flaky Skin

Dry flaky skin occurs when the skin loses moisture, leading to irritation, tightness, and peeling. This is often caused by environmental factors, underlying conditions, or improper skincare.

Common Causes:

  • Cold or dry weather
  • Excessive washing or harsh soaps
  • Dehydration
  • Skin conditions like eczema or psoriasis
  • Vitamin deficiencies

Treatment for Dry Flaky Skin:

Treat dry skin by moisturizing regularly, using gentle cleansers, and avoiding hot water. Drinking plenty of water and using a humidifier can also help. Seek medical advice for persistent symptoms.
