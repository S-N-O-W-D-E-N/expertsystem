Greasy, Flaky Skin on Your Scalp or Face

Greasy, flaky skin on the scalp or face is often caused by conditions such as seborrheic dermatitis, dandruff, or other skin irritations. This condition is typically characterized by greasy patches of skin accompanied by flakes of dead skin.

Common Causes:

  • Seborrheic dermatitis (skin condition that causes red, scaly patches)
  • Dandruff (flaky, greasy scalp skin)
  • Acne (sometimes on the forehead or face)
  • Hormonal imbalances (which can increase oil production)
  • Stress or environmental factors (which can worsen conditions)

Treatment for Greasy, Flaky Skin on Scalp or Face:

Treatment options include using medicated shampoos for dandruff or seborrheic dermatitis, avoiding harsh skincare products, applying moisturizers, and possibly using topical treatments prescribed by a dermatologist. It's important to follow a consistent skincare regimen.
