Intense Itching

Intense itching is a symptom characterized by an uncontrollable urge to scratch, often caused by conditions like allergies, skin irritations, or insect bites.

Common Causes:

  • Allergic reactions
  • Insect bites or stings
  • Skin conditions like eczema or psoriasis
  • Dry skin
  • Exposure to irritants (chemicals, plants, etc.)

Treatment for Intense Itching:

Relief can include using antihistamines, applying moisturizers, or consulting a healthcare provider for persistent symptoms. Avoid scratching to prevent further irritation.
